Who are we



Cogentix is Niche IT Consulting and Software Development company helping customers accelerate & innovate their software development lifecylce.

Software innovation is the differentiating ingredient for all businesses today. Some of new trends like Adoption of cloud, microservices, API-led connectivity, containerization, automation of CI/CD and DevSecOps have increased the complexity of engineering software products.

Cogentix as an idea was formed to help customers in the transformation of their software products. We help customers to build scale, enhance efficiency and reduce overall Total Cost of ownership.​

We work in true partner mode as an extended team to customer's team. we firmly believe in innovation and co-creating ideas togather with our customer.
